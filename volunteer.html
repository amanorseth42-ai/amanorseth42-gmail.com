Get Involved

Give a Saturday. Change a shoreline.

No experience required — just a willingness to get your hands a little sandy and muddy for a good cause.

Opportunities

Ways you can help

1

Mangrove Planting

Half-day plantings on Saturdays at Aboadze and Whin Estuary. Boots provided.

2

Beach & Creek Clean-ups

Monthly clean-ups along Sekondi beach front, gloves and bags supplied.

3

School Climate Clubs

Assist facilitators running after-school climate sessions in local schools.

4

Admin & Outreach

Help with record-keeping, social media or community mobilisation from home.
