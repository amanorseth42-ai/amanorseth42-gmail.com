Support Our Work

Your gift keeps the tide turning.

Every cedi funds seedlings, boreholes, classroom sessions and safer livelihoods along the coast.

Why give

Small gifts, lasting shoreline

Anidaso Green Initiative runs on community labour and modest, well-tracked funding. A single mangrove seedling costs less than a bottle of water to plant — but a hectare of restored mangrove can protect an entire village from flooding for decades.

Donations directly fund seedlings and nursery upkeep, borehole parts and water testing, teaching materials for climate clubs, and safety equipment for clean-up volunteers. We publish a simple impact report to every donor twice a year.
